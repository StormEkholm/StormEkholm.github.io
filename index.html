about

a little about us

HIGGINS is a Barbershop for both guys and gals.

The HIGGINS 'barbershop experience' is a sophisticated experience mixed up with old school qualities with a pinch of Rock 'n' Roll.

Our inspiration is taken from the kustom culture, meaning that we believe in individualism. Kustom culture is when you take a car or a motorcycle or anything and make it uniquely your own. Therefore becoming completely individual and custom to you.

Together with Hair Construction Salon & Academies I teach and develop hairdressers. Hair Construction Salon & Academies is a team of international hairdressers that gather twice a year to create a style book of the trends and a teaching guide for all hairdressers to use in their salons and to inspire hairdressers and clients alike..

Coming all the way from the USA to bring you an international experience of style, technique and service. I have used the past 14 years developing and perfecting my skills to make sure you shine and stay sharp! Every customer, cut and shave is unique. A HIGGINS EXPERIENCE
